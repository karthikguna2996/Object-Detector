Real Time Object Detection

Before starting, please wait for the model to fully load. Once the model is ready,.
click the button to enable the webcam and start detecting objects in real-time .
The webcam view will appear, allowing you to see and interact with the detected objects.

To see objects classified in real-time, hold them close to your webcam.
When you're ready, click "Enable Webcam" below and allow the browser to access your
webcam when prompted (look for the permission request at the top left of your window).
